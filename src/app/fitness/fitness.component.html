Welcome to my fitness page

In 2002 after returning from London I decided to try my hand at fitness having grown up playing lots of football and cricket. I got my first job at a local YMCA and over the next few years worked towards my group fitness and fitness instructor certificates.

In 2011 and 2013 I had my proudest moments in fitness taking away Group Fitness Instructor of the Year at the New Zealand Fitness Industry Awards.

These days I am a passionate Zumba Fitness instructor doing a few classes a week but always on the look out for more.

New Zealand Fitness Industry Awards

2013 Winner Group Exercise Award

The palpable group energy evident before, during and after Brent’s classes demonstrate how participants simply love what Brent does and how he does it! Our specialist group exercise judges noted Brent’s technical expertise and communication performance was exemplary

2012 Finalist Group Exercise Award

Unfortunately in 2012 despite being a finalist for both freestyle and pre-chorographed categories I was unable to come away with a win. However it did give me more motivation for 2013

2011 Winner Group Exercise Award

This is the first year that a Zumba instructor has entered the awards and Brent was one of many Zumba instructors to enter. Zumba instruction is unique as a group exercise format in that communication occurs non-verbally using body language, energy and expression: aspects our specialist judges agreed Brent demonstrated at award winning level
